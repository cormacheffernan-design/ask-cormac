## What is established so far
- FundEQ is an active fund-management platform product in the Finplex / FintureAI world.
- It is the rolling wholesale & institutional capital that funds the FintureAI market maker's liquidity fund — providing the dry powder for underwriting and the aftermarket bid.
- As a constantly rolling fund, its capital tenor is matched to the asset tenor by design: rolling capital continuously replenishes dry powder, and staggered rolls mean redemptions trickle rather than spike. This roll tempo mirrors the ~6-month deal recycle horizon.
- Capital in the fund is equity-at-risk: it earns the spread in good times and wears credit losses in bad. It is valued at a daily NAV (cash + inventory at last traded price or live-quote mid).
