# FintureAI — brand & cash management trust

- FintureAI is a commercial brand / origination & distribution layer operating as a CAR under Sapien Capital Partners. It is not a separate licensee.
- Three-layer model: FintureAI (origination/distribution) → Finplex Nerve Centre (technology) → Finplex Fund umbrella MIS (regulated/legal).
- Central liquidity product: the Cash Management Trust (Cash Pool) — places wholesale investor cash into diversified term deposits with Australian mutual ADIs via Moroku's hub-and-spoke API, settling via the Westpac CMT.
- The market is open to FintureAI wholesale clients. Most buy and hold to maturity, receiving distributions along the way.
